Our services

We offer one on one and group tutoring services to leaners of different phases. From Foundation (grade 1) to Senior phase grade (grade 7) ,we only help/assist our leaners with homeworks in all subjects except home languages (HL's come at an extra cost). Grade 8&9 we have Mathematics which is attended during the week based on our schedule. FET phase we have all subjects, Upgrading students are also welcome.
